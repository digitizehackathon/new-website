Digitize Hackathon



Digitize Hackathon is a free 24 hour coding event for high school students in the Bay Area. We specifically focus on our three fundamental themes: Environmental Conservation, Health & Wellness, and Social Welfare, to encourage students to use technology for social good and create projects that impact the community.
